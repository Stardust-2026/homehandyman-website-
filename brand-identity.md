# HomeHandyman Brand Identity

## Logo Concept

### Visual Identity
- **Icon**: A stylized house silhouette with a wrench integrated into the roofline
- **Style**: Clean, modern, geometric
- **Symbolism**: House = home, Wrench = handyman services
- **Alternative**: A hand holding a house (representing care and service)

### Logo Variations
1. **Primary**: Full logo with icon + "HomeHandyman" wordmark
2. **Icon Only**: For favicon, app icon, social media avatars
3. **Horizontal**: Icon left, text right (for headers)
4. **Stacked**: Icon above text (for square applications)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter or Poppins (clean, modern, highly readable)
- **Weight**: Semi-bold for headlines, regular for body

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional yet approachable
- Confident but not arrogant
- Helpful and solution-oriented
- Local and relatable (Singapore context)

### Key Messages
1. **Vetted Professionals**: Every handyman is background-checked and skilled
2. **Fast Response**: Book in 60 seconds, confirmed within 30 minutes
3. **Quality Guaranteed**: We stand behind every job
4. **Transparent Pricing**: No hidden fees, ever

---

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Photography
- Real Singapore homes (HDB, condo, landed)
- Diverse handymen at work
- Before/after shots where applicable
- Bright, natural lighting

### Icons
- Line-style icons for services
- Consistent 2px stroke width
- Rounded corners for friendliness

### Buttons
- Primary: Trust Blue with white text
- Secondary: White with Trust Blue border
- CTA: Warm Orange for high-contrast actions
