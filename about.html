About HomeHandyman

Connecting Singapore homeowners with skilled, vetted handymen. Built on trust, quality, and community.

Our Story

HomeHandyman was born from a simple frustration: finding a reliable handyman in Singapore shouldn't be so hard.

We've all been there — a leaking pipe at midnight, a broken aircon in the sweltering heat, or a door that just won't close right. You ask friends for recommendations, scroll through endless listings, and still end up wondering if the person showing up at your door is trustworthy and skilled.

That's why we created HomeHandyman. We believe every homeowner deserves access to quality, vetted professionals who show up on time, do the job right, and treat your home with respect.

And for the skilled tradespeople who keep our homes running? We believe they deserve fair pay, steady work, and the chance to build a reputation that speaks for itself.

Our Mission

To make home maintenance effortless for Singapore homeowners while empowering skilled tradespeople to build thriving businesses.

Our Vision

To be Singapore's most trusted platform for home services — where quality meets convenience, and every job is done right.
